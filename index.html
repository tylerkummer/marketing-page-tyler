Technology Collecting Dust?

Have any unwanted tech lying around the house you don't want to sell? Buy a very expensive camera and only use it for special ocassions? Or maybe you are someone who is in need of camera equipment for a wedding or graduation but do not want to spend a heft price for a one time use electronic. Well Use My Tech Stuff is an off hand rental service where anyone can post any technology equipment they have while someone else can request to rent that equipment.

One Time Event!

A perfect service for people that are looking to use expensive equipment for a one time use! Simply search through our catalog of potential renters in your area, negotiate a price, and request to rent their equipment for however long is agreed upon. No middle man required, a simple service between someone willing to rent out their equiment and someone willing to rent.

Anything Ya Got!

The equipment a user can post to be rented can range from anything from laptop to camera and everything in between. Even an old DOS computer or a game console or anything any users in your area are willing to let be used.

Sign Up!

Simply create an account and specify if you are either renting or seeking to rent technological equipment. Post photos of the item before the rental and post images after the rental. If anything is lost or damaged we will charge the renter for the repair or replacement cost of the equipment.
